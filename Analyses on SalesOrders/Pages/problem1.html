Question

"How many orders have been palced per month from the earliest date to the last? Return a row for every month and year shown in the database."

Solution

select year(OrderDate) [Year] ,
		datename(month, OrderDate) [Month], 
		count(salesorderid) [Orders]
from sales.SalesOrderHeader
group by datename(month, OrderDate), year(OrderDate), datepart(month, OrderDate)
order by year(orderdate), datepart(month, OrderDate)

Proof Query

select count(*) 
from sales.SalesOrderHeader soh
where datepart(month, soh.OrderDate) = 7
and datepart(year, soh.OrderDate) = 2005

Explaination

In the following query, it queries the rows from the sales.SalesOrderHeader table. Then, it groups the orders by year and month. Additionally, the select statment returns for each group, the orderdate year, month, and the total count per month. Finally, sorts the rows by year, then by month.
